What We Do

We provide most promising startups in СEE countries and Asia Pacific with

Growth hacking

We achieve outsized returns by accessing the best deals and actively helping our portfolio companies reach their full potential. Our active involvement gives us a deep understanding of our portfolio companies, enabling us to eliminate problems before they occur.

Scaling returns

We target technology-driven business poised for immediate and rapid growth starting at the seed stage. We select companies who match our specific strengths and leverage the expertise of our Partners and advisors, fast-tracking them to success.

Innovation framework

We act as a Mentors for our portfolio companies and connect them to our business network and industry advisors. We invest time to help our portfolio companies with their business development and operational needs.

Starting with a network-driven selection process, we identify transformational technology startups and invest earlier than traditional VCs to maximize our influence. With this position, we support our portfolio companies with advisory and hands-on guidance.
